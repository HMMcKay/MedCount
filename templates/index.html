              Medication Lookup

              Online lookup fallback
              Quick add uses a built-in local database first (covers ~98% of common medications, no network use). When off (default), an unrecognized medication just gets left for you to fill in by hand.

              May leak: the medication text you typed (e.g. "Seroquel 200mg") is sent over the network to the NIH's public RxNorm lookup service.
              Never sent: your PIN, dose history, quantities on hand, prescriber/pharmacy info, or any other field.
              Returned & used: only a standardized drug name, form, and strength — to fill the form, nothing more.
